About Us

About The Event

Deep Dive is a combination of breakthrough brainstorming, deep analysis and business coaching, focusing on four key areas:

  • Strategy

  • Organization

  • Lifestyle

  • Executive Presence

  • Finance

Usually held in a quiet and rejuvenating environment, Deep Dive takes you deep into your business and helps you uncover potential opportunities in your market, people and your capabilities. The off-site experience enables you to view your health and your business through fresh eyes, in a quiet environment with the goal of coming up with a clear action plan to tackle problems and find new opportunities. The objective of the Deep Dive sessions is to develop new paths, map timelines, determine significant touch points and identify key ‘actors’ (people) that will ultimately become the backbone of our implementation plan. Execution is the sole aim of the program.

Benefits of the Deep Dive Approach:

  • A structured process with practical tools to analyze your key business areas

  • Group learning with other business owners facing similar challenges

  • Only one participant per business type to ensure a safe peer learning environment without competition

  • The opportunity to present your product/services to your peers

  • The opportunity to see your business through fresh eyes and find new perspectives

  • A clear plan of action, with implementation support from the Deep Dive team for three months
